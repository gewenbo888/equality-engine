# Equality Engine · 平等引擎

A civilization-scale, fully bilingual (English / 中文) exploration of equality, inequality, hierarchy, justice, rights, power, opportunity and the coordination systems that hold complex societies together. Equality is treated not as a slogan but as a deep structural problem spanning biology, psychology, economics, law, technology and governance.

Ten interactive systems — from the egalitarian forager band through caste, democracy, capitalism and social democracy to AI-governed futures — plus a seven-axis **Civilizational Equality** meta-model and a recursive future simulator. A live `EN | 中文` toggle switches every word, label and axis without reload.

## Systems

1. The Origin of Equality — an equality-index curve across history
2. Biology, Hierarchy & Human Nature — the egalitarian↔despotic species spectrum
3. Justice, Rights & Law — the widening circle of rights (ratchet ladder)
4. Economic Equality & Wealth — live Lorenz curve, Gini coefficient & redistribution engine
5. Education, Opportunity & Mobility — an intergenerational mobility (Markov) simulator
6. Technology, AI & Digital Inequality — an algorithmic-fairness dashboard (parity vs accuracy)
7. The Civilization Comparison Engine — Athens · Rome · Imperial China · Islamic Caliphates · Medieval Europe · Modern democracy
8. The Psychology of Fairness & Resentment — the Ultimatum Game
9. Future Equality Systems — a recursive nine-layer equality simulator
10. The Unified Equality Model — a seven-axis radar over eight societies

**Meta-model:** Civilizational Equality = Fair Opportunity + Power Distribution + Legal Protection + Social Mobility + Information Access + Economic Participation + Dignity Preservation.
